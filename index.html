Butter Rice

This golden butter rice, flavored with ginger and turmeric, is a forgotten way of cooking butter rice. It isn't cooked rice with butter stirred in , it really is rice cooked in butter. This is my favorite version, with seasonings inspired by a classic Indian drink called golden milk .

      steps

  1. Melt butter in a pan or pot with a tight-fitting lid over medium heat. As soon as butter starts to bubble.
  2. add in ginger, turmeric, cayenne, brown sugar, and salt. Cook, whisking, for 1 minute.
  3. Add milk and cook, whisking, for 1 minute.
  4. Add water and cook, whisking, for 1 minute.
  5. Add rice and cook, stirring, for 1 minute.
  6. Add oil and cook, stirring, for 1 minute.
